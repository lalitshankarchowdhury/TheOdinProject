Simple Strawberry Syrup

Use this strawberry syrup to make natural-flavored beverages. This simple recipe came from an attempt to create a more natural substitute for the strawberry syrup sold in stores. I even use it in my own beverages!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups quartered strawberries

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine water and sugar in a saucepan over medium-high heat; stir until sugar is dissolved. Mix strawberries into saucepan and bring to a boil; boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer until strawberries are mushy and sauce is thick, about 10 minutes. Strain liquid into a bottle and refrigerate.
