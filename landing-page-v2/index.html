This is Inter.
Inter is a typeface carefully crafted & designed for computer screens.
Practical and elegant
Your go-to font for reports, signboards, and user-interfaces. Inter fits everywhere, emphasizing form and function.
Minimal yet sophisticated
Equipped with 2548 glyphs, 18 styles, and 33 OpenType features, Inter is not your typical sans-serif typeface.
Thick and thin
Inter comes with 9 weights, from 100 through 900, with support for italics.
